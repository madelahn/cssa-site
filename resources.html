Resources
General educational resources to help with your cybersecurity journey!

This page will regularly be updated with new materials, so if you have anything you'd like to add, send them to vp@cyber.skule.ca or our Discord server!

General
Introduction to Cybersecurity - A detailed and comprehensive starting point for those wanting to learn more about the field.
Awesome Security - A large collection of a variety of resources for different fields of cybersecurity. Includes information on networking, DevOps, exploitation, and more!
Cybrary - An online learning platform offering free and paid online courses for skills and career paths, as well as certification preparations.
TryHackMe/HackTheBox - A browser-based cybersecurity training platform for all skill levels and fields. HackTheBox additionally offers hands-on learning with their virtual labs.
Penetration Testing
Awesome Penetration Testing - A collection of resources for testing for vulnerabilities (simulating cyberattacks) on computer systems or applications.
CTFLearn - A hacking/CTF (capture-the-flag) learning resource and community, targeted at supporting beginners.
PicoCTF - An educational program offering educational content and CTF puzzles for all skill levels. It has a large archive of challenges, and has a yearly live competition.
OSINT/Forensics
Awesome Forensics - A library of resources for forensic analysis/investigation tools; i.e., being able to acquire and process data in digital devices.
Awesome OSINT - A library of resources and tools pertaining Open-Source Intelligence (similar to forensics, but uses publicly available sources).
OSINT Techniques: Resources for Uncovering Online Information - The 'holy grail' of OSINT; a comprehensive guide of techniques and resources for uncovering data online.
Career Development
Guide to Finding a Job in Tech - An article that offers some good insight on applying to jobs, from resume and cover letter-building to the interview process. Applies to any role in technology.
r/ITCareerQuestions Wiki - A general guide to getting a job in Information Technologies. Reddit actually offers a lot of great resources and feedback, so I recommend taking a look and/or asking questions to members of the Subreddit.
SANS Overview of Cybersecurity Roles - A list of 20 roles you can have in the cybersecurity field, with a description of what the role looks like, relevant courses/certifications you should take, and how the role is relevant in the real world.
Cybersecurity Certification Roadmap - Cybersecurity often require gaining certificates to further your career. This is a great visual guide to understand the types of certificates you should be aiming for depending on the role you are pursuing.
