About Us
The University of Toronto Cybersecurity Student Association is a student-run organisation dedicated to promoting knowledge in the field of cybersecurity! Our mission is to unite students with similar passions and drive meaningful growth in their career through our workshops, events, and industry partners.
  • Education
    To teach cybersecurity concepts and technical skills to students of any discipline and experience level.
  • Community
    To collaborate, engage, and support other passionate students in cybersecurity.
  • Awareness
    To play a role in raising cybersecurity awareness within the University as well as the external community.
