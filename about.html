About Us
The University of Toronto Cybersecurity Student Association (CSSA) is a student-run organisation dedicated to promoting knowledge and awareness to the field of cybersecurity.

CSSA aims to contribute to student life by connecting students with others passionate about developing their cybersecurity skills and introducing them to opportunities within the cybersecurity field. Ultimately, we hope to foster interest in cybersecurity and mentor the next generation of leaders in the field!
